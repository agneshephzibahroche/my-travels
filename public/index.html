How to get your SimplyGo statement ▾
  1. Sign in to your SimplyGo account on the SimplyGo website or app.
  2. Open your card or account transaction history.
  3. Look for the monthly transit statement or statement download option.
  4. Download the statement as a `PDF` if available.
  5. Come back here and upload that statement file.

If SimplyGo only gives you text-based exports, you can upload a `.txt` statement too.
